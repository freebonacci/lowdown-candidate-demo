The role

Fintra is seeking a senior growth leader to take ownership of acquisition and retention as the business scales toward a Series C raise. The successful candidate will work closely with the CEO and product leadership to drive performance across paid, organic, and partnership channels, and will inherit a capable team ready for senior direction.

Responsibilities
  • Own the full growth model — acquisition, activation, and retention
  • Scale paid acquisition across Google and Meta; identify and test new channels
  • Lead and develop a team of three growth specialists
  • Partner with product on onboarding flows and lifecycle communications
  • Report directly to the CEO; present growth strategy to the board on a quarterly basis
The team

The growth team comprises three direct reports: a paid media specialist with two years at Fintra, a content lead with a strong organic and SEO background, and a data analyst who joined from Revolut six months ago. The team is technically capable and operationally experienced; the priority hire is a senior leader who can set strategic direction and drive accountability.

What we're looking for
  • A demonstrable track record of scaling B2C growth at a technology company
  • Strong commercial judgement and confidence working with unit economics
  • Experience building and managing high-performing growth teams
  • Comfort operating in ambiguous environments and building process from scratch
  • Based in or willing to commute to London (Shoreditch) three to four days per week
Interview process
  • Introductory call with the talent team (30 minutes)
  • Growth strategy conversation with the CEO (60 minutes)
  • Paid take-home growth audit (two to three hours)
  • Panel with the product and data leads (60 minutes)
  • Offer issued within five working days of the final interview
